Her Name is Pearly

Her Personality

She is very indepentant and prefers to be alone. She often runs on her wheel with her mouth full of food! She squeeks when she is sad or scared (can only be head when you put your ear close to her). She likes to make a ticking sound when she is upset or fustrated (very cute in a way!). She accepts cuddles but only from people she is comfortable with. She likes to run around the house and explore. She is very clean and spends a lot of time grooming and cleaning herself.
