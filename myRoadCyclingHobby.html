My bike: The Giant TCR 2022

I quite like this bike but this bike size is perfect comfort but a size too big for the racing feel. For my next bike, I'm thinking of getting a TIME bike instead as they are made better in terms of engineering and quality.

Here are some details of my bike:
  • Tires: GP 5000 25mm clincher
  • Size: L
  • Groupset: Shimano R7000 11S 105
  • Chain-rings: 50-34T
  • Cassete: 11-34T
  • Drop-bar width: 40cm
  • Saddle: Prolog NTR
  • Wheels: Dura-ace C24 9100 wheels
  • Weight: 7.8kg
